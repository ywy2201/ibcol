International Blockchain Olympiad

The International Blockchain Olympiads are the Olympic games for Blockchain enthusiast

Are you a student, a team or a startup with a promising idea for the blockchain industry? Then present your idea at the International Blockchain Olympiad in front of various blockchain specialists and potentially win cash prizes and professional mentorship.

Who?

There are 2 channels of entry:
1. Students (must have valid student ID)
2. Open to Public

What?

A competition designed to challenge blockchain enthusiasts to come up with solutions and use cases for the revolutionary technology.

When?

Initial Stage: June 24-26th 2019
Final Stage: July 12-13th 2019

Where?

The final short listed contestants will present their project live in the final stage, which will be held in Hong Kong.

Why?

To connect people that share the same passion for blockchain and to inject growth to potential talent by rewarding the winner with US $25,000 to kick start their project.

How?

1. Click on the "Participate" below to submit your team info and whitepaper.

Participate

2. You will be notified if your has been shortlisted during the initial stage by June 24th 2019.

3. If you made it to the final stage you will be asked to present your project in Hong Kong on July 19th 2019.
